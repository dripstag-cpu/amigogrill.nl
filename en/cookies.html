Cookie Policy

Which cookies we use, what they're for, and how to manage them.

Last updated: January 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to make the site work, remember your preferences, or analyse usage. They are not harmful in themselves, but certain cookies may by law only be placed with your consent.

2. Which cookies does Amigo Grill use?

We try to use as few cookies as possible. Here's an overview:

Name / source Purpose Type Retention
amigogrill.nl — session Keeps the website working correctly (for example, remembering language or menu preference). Functional (essential) Session
amigogrill.nl — cookie-consent Remembers your choice from the cookie banner. Functional (essential) 12 months
TheFork / LaFourchette Embeds the booking widget. See TheFork's own cookie policy. Functional / analytics (third party) Variable (per TheFork)
Google Maps Displays the map on the contact page. Functional (third party) Variable (per Google)
Google Fonts Loads webfonts for typography. Functional (third party) Variable

We do not place tracking cookies for personalised advertising. If this changes, we'll update this list and the consent options accordingly.

3. How do I give consent?

On your first visit you'll see a cookie banner where you can choose which categories you accept. Functional cookies are always active — they're needed for the site to work. Analytical or marketing cookies are only placed after you explicitly agree.

4. How do I manage or delete cookies?

You can refuse, delete, or change your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari (macOS)
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: if you block all cookies, parts of the site may stop working, such as the booking module or the map view.

5. Third parties

Some cookies are placed by third parties (TheFork, Google). Their use is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies:

  • TheFork privacy policy
  • Google privacy policy

6. Changes

This cookie policy may be updated if our practice or legislation changes. The most recent version is always on this page.

7. Questions

Questions about cookies or privacy? Email us at info@amigogrill.nl or call +31 20 845 1081. See also our privacy policy.
