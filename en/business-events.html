Frequently asked questions

About private events and groups

How many guests can you host for a private event?+

From 8 to 60 guests. For larger groups we can reserve part of the restaurant or the entire private room.

Do you offer a set menu for groups?+

Yes. We work with set menus per person, including starters, tableside grill and dessert. A drinks package is optional. Prices on request, depending on group size and menu choice.

How does the quote work?+

Email prinsengracht@amigogrill.nl with date, time, number of guests and any wishes. One event manager responds within 4 working hours with a detailed quote and availability.

Do I need to pay a deposit?+

For groups of 10 or more we sometimes ask for confirmation or a deposit, especially on weekends or in peak season. This is clearly stated in the quote.

Can you accommodate allergies and dietary requirements?+

Yes. With your final guest list, let us know about allergies or dietary requirements (vegetarian, gluten-free, halal on request). We provide proper alternatives without making anyone feel singled out.

How late can we stay?+

The restaurant closes at 00:00 (Friday and Saturday 01:00). For private room hire on closing days or late into the night, we'll arrange this with you.
