Why Amigo Grill

Three reasons to dine with us

01

Beef we import ourselves

Our tenderloin doesn't pass through a wholesale warehouse. We've worked with the same Argentinian importer for more than a decade, sourcing directly from the Pampas. A fresh shipment arrives every week, with no middlemen and no doubt about its origin.

02

Warm until the last bite

The tableside grill isn't a gimmick. It's how Argentinians have eaten for generations. We bring your bife de lomo on glowing coals to your table. Your meat stays pink while you keep talking. No one has to rush.

03

Romantic canal-side dining

Three minutes' walk from the Anne Frank House, in the heart of the canal belt. Views of the Westerkerk, four-hundred-year-old facades. One of the few places in central Amsterdam to genuinely dine right on the water without a tourist queue.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

Where is Amigo Grill located in Amsterdam?+

Amigo Grill is at Prinsengracht 188 in Amsterdam, three minutes' walk from the Anne Frank House and right in the canal belt. Tram and metro stop Westermarkt is one minute away.

Is it an authentic Argentinian restaurant?+

Yes. We've worked with the same Argentinian beef importer since 2011 and serve food on a tableside grill (parrilla) the way it's done in Buenos Aires. Bife de lomo, bife de chorizo, chorizos criollos and empanadas are on the menu.

What is a tableside grill or parrilla?+

A tableside grill (parrilla in Argentinian) is a small grill with glowing charcoal served right at your table. The meat stays warm from the first bite to the last. It's how Argentinians have eaten steak for a hundred years.

Do I need to book at Amigo Grill?+

For the indoor restaurant, booking online via TheFork is the fastest. For a terrace table we'd love a phone call on +31 20 845 1081 so we can place you right by the water. On weekends we recommend booking at least a week ahead.

When is the canal terrace open?+

The terrace is open from mid-April to late October, weather permitting. Indoors, you can dine year-round with canal views through large windows.

What are Amigo Grill's opening hours?+

Daily from 12:00 to 00:00. Friday and Saturday until 01:00. The kitchen closes about 60 minutes before closing time.

Do you host business events and group dinners?+

Yes. From 8 to 60 guests, with tableside parrilla and a private dining room with screen for presentations, quiz nights or live sport. One event manager, response within 4 working hours.

How much does dinner at Amigo Grill cost?+

Mains start around €26 for a bife de chorizo and from €30 for a bife de lomo. The Gran Parrillada for two is €86. Starters like chorizos criollos and empanadas start at €9.75.
